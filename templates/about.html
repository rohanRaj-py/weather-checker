Understanding weather and its benefits

🌦️ What is Weather?

Weather refers to the atmospheric conditions in a specific location at a specific time. It includes elements such as temperature, humidity, rainfall, wind, and cloudiness. Weather is dynamic and changes frequently.

🌍 Importance of Weather Forecasting

  • 🚜 Helps farmers plan agricultural activities.
  • ✈️ Supports safe air and sea navigation.
  • 🚧 Assists in disaster management during storms, floods, or cyclones.
  • 🏖️ Helps people plan travel and outdoor activities.
  • ⚡ Supports energy management and electricity usage predictions.
